Story Engagement Tips

• Post during peak hours (7-9 PM)
• Use interactive elements
• Keep text readable and concise
• Add relevant hashtags and locations

💡 Instagram Story Ideas & Best Practices

🎨 Content Ideas

  • Behind the scenes: Show your process and workspace
  • Day in the life: Share your daily routine
  • Quick tips: Educational content in bite-sized format
  • User-generated content: Repost customer stories
  • Product demos: Show products in action

📊 Engagement Strategies

  • Use polls and questions: Get direct feedback
  • Create countdowns: Build anticipation
  • Share user mentions: Build community
  • Add music and GIFs: Make stories more dynamic
  • Use story highlights: Save important content

📱 Technical Tips

  • Optimal size: 1080x1920 pixels (9:16 ratio)
  • Text placement: Keep important text in safe zones
  • Story length: 15 seconds max for videos
  • Consistent branding: Use brand colors and fonts
  • Call-to-action: Always include next steps
