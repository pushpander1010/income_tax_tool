💡 Instagram Hashtag Best Practices

📊 Optimal Hashtag Strategy

  • Use 20-30 hashtags: Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post
  • Mix popularity levels: 70% niche, 20% medium, 10% popular
  • Research competitors: See what hashtags work in your niche
  • Create branded hashtags: Build your own community hashtag
  • Track performance: Monitor which hashtags drive engagement

🎯 Hashtag Categories

  • Niche hashtags: Specific to your content (10K-100K posts)
  • Community hashtags: Connect with like-minded users
  • Location hashtags: Target local audience
  • Trending hashtags: Ride current trends (use sparingly)
  • Branded hashtags: Your unique brand identifier

⚠️ Hashtag Don'ts

  • Avoid banned hashtags: Can shadowban your content
  • Don't use irrelevant tags: Hurts engagement rate
  • Avoid overly popular tags: Your post gets lost quickly
  • Don't repeat same set: Instagram may flag as spam
  • Skip broken hashtags: Check if hashtags are working
