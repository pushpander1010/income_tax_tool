How to use this GST Calculator

  • Enter the amount, select GST rate (or Custom), and choose whether your amount is inclusive of GST.
  • Pick Intra-state to see CGST+SGST split (equal halves), or Inter-state for IGST.
  • Change rounding to match your invoicing (paise vs nearest ₹1).
  • Use Share to copy a link with your inputs, and Download CSV for records.
